ABOUT KIMS

KIMS Hospitals is one of the largest corporate healthcare groups in India, with hospitals in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and Maharashtra. We provide multi-disciplinary integrated healthcare services, with a focus on tertiary and quaternary healthcare at an affordable cost.

Our network of 12 hospitals and 4000 beds is spread across multiple cities. We offer a bouquet of comprehensive healthcare services across 25 specialties, including:

  • Cardiac Sciences
  • Oncology
  • Neurosciences
  • Gastric Sciences
  • Orthopedics
  • Organ Transplantation
  • Renal Sciences
  • Mother & Child Care
